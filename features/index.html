Blinky Features

Blinky provides simple, focused modules that enhance gameplay while keeping performance impact low.

Blink (Fake Lag)

Temporarily stops movement packets from being sent to the server while allowing free movement on the client. When disabled, all queued packets are sent at once, causing the server to process them instantly and teleport the player.

ESP

Highlights players through blocks and obstacles, providing constant awareness of nearby entities and player positions.

Player Size Changer

Adjusts the visual size of player models, improving visibility or allowing customized in-game appearance preferences.

No Fog

Removes in-game fog effects entirely, ensuring clear visibility across all environments and distances.

Rainbow Sky

Applies a dynamic rainbow color cycle to the sky, creating a unique and visually distinct in-game atmosphere.

Autoclicker

Automatically performs mouse clicks at configurable speeds, helping maintain consistent clicking without manual input.

Lightweight Loader

Designed with performance in mind, Blinky uses minimal system resources and avoids unnecessary background processes for smooth gameplay.

Feature behavior may vary depending on server configuration, latency, and Minecraft version. Use responsibly and at your own risk.
